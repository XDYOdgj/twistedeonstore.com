48 Candle Dust Covers for Perfect Protection & Elegance

These 48 candle dust covers are an essential addition to your candle-making toolkit, designed to keep your candles clean and dust-free at every stage. Made from high-quality 250GSM white cardstock, they are built to last and can be reused multiple times, ensuring value for your craft. Each cover comes with a precisely sized oblong hole, making it easy to insert and position the wick accurately.

Whether you're creating candles for personal use or preparing them for sale, these dust covers offer both functionality and a touch of class.
