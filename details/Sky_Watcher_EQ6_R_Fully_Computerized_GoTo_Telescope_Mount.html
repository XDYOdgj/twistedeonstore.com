Discover the Sky-Watcher EQ6-R: A Powerful and Versatile Telescope Mount

Designed for both visual stargazing and astrophotography, the Sky-Watcher EQ6-R combines advanced technology with durable engineering to deliver an unmatched observational experience.

Unmatched Precision with a Computerized GoTo System

The Sky-Watcher EQ6-R features a fully computerized GoTo telescope mount that guides you effortlessly to thousands of celestial objects. Whether you're new to astronomy or an experienced observer, this system simplifies navigation and enhances your experience of the night sky.

Smooth and Quiet Operation with Belt-Driven Motors

Equipped with belt-driven stepper motors, the EQ6-R ensures silent and smooth slewing, minimizing periodic error for more accurate tracking. This makes it perfect for detailed visual observation and long exposure astrophotography sessions.

Built for Durability and Stability

Constructed from robust all-metal components, the Sky-Watcher EQ6-R offers excellent durability and a powerful 44-pound payload capacity. This makes it suitable for a wide range of telescope setups, including refractors and larger Dobsonian telescopes.

Quick Setup and Easy Transport

Its lightweight yet sturdy design includes a built-in handle, making it easy to transport and set up anywhere. Whether you're stargazing from your backyard or embarking on a more advanced observational trip, the EQ6-R provides versatility and convenience.

Accurate Polar Alignment Made Simple

An illuminated polar finder scope is included to simplify the polar alignment process, ensuring your mount tracks celestial objects accurately across a wide latitude range. This feature reduces setup time and enhances your stargazing experience.

Endless Stargazing Possibilities

With the SynScan hand controller, the Sky-Watcher EQ6-R grants access to a comprehensive database of over 42,900 celestial objects. This interactive tool empowers you to explore the universe with confidence and ease, adding a new dimension to your observational adventures.

Why Choose the Sky-Watcher EQ6-R?

The Sky-Watcher EQ6-R delivers a perfect balance of power, precision, and portability. Whether you're capturing images of distant galaxies or simply enjoying the beauty of the night sky, this telescope mount is a top choice for serious stargazers and photography enthusiasts.
